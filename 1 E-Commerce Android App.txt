1 E-Commerce Android App

a. Overview

This Android app aims to provide users with a seamless e-commerce experience featuring login, sign-up, and a product page. The app utilizes Firebase for database management to store customer data securely.

b. Technologies Implemented

- Android Studio for app development
- Firebase for database connectivity

c. App Activities

	1. **Login Activity:**
   		- Fields: Username, Password
   		- Actions: Login button

	2. **Sign-Up Activity:**
   		- Fields: Username, Email, Password, Confirm Password
   		- Actions: Sign-Up button

	3. **Product Page Activity:**
   		- Display: List of products with details
   		- Actions: View product details, Add to cart
